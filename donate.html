*Please note that Zelle only supports transfers between US-based accounts.

Zelle is a convenient, secure way to make bank-to-bank transfers. Check out this link to see if see if your bank participates.

If you already know that your bank uses Zelle, then you can send a gift directly from your bank’s own app or online portal. Just open Zelle and send the money to ariellelegere@gmail.com.

If your bank DOES NOT automatically use Zelle to send money, you can create a Zelle account and send the money with no fees. To set up Zelle, you must have a Visa® or MasterCard® debit card associated with a US-based account. They do not accept debit cards associated with international accounts or any credit cards. To get started setting up a Zelle account, click HERE.

For existing PayPal users: clicking on the donate button below will take you to the secure PayPal transaction site. You will then have the option of logging into your PayPal account after entering your selected donation amount. There is no charge to you for this service, unless you opt to connect your credit card.
