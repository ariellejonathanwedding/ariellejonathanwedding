How do I get to and from the venue?

For those traveling within NY/NJ, public transportation is a good option. If you’re coming from Brooklyn or lower Manhattan, you can take the PATH train to Journal Square and easily connect to the 87 bus line. Alternatively, if you’re coming from Midtown or upper Manhattan, you can take the 119 bus from Port Authority on 42 St.

For those in Jersey City & Hoboken, it’s a reasonable cab/Uber ride, and public transportation is also available.

If you’re feeling adventurous, there are also bike share services available nearly all in the surrounding areas!

What local attractions do you recommend seeing while we’re in town?

Jersey City is across the river from New York City, so there are quite a lot of things to do!

Here are some of our overall favorites:
- See a Broadway show (download the TodayTix app to find discounted seats).
- Explore Central Park.
- Visit one of the many museums. Several offer free or discounted visits (some on certain days), so be sure to take a look online ahead of time.
- Enjoy some Jazz at a fancy venue like Birdland, The Iridium, or Jazz at Lincoln Center.
- Catch more casual live music at East Village Social, Fat Black Pussycat, Pianos, or The Bowery Electric.

Locally in the Jersey City area, we like to:
- Walk along the Hoboken waterfront for a view of the New York skyline (especially at night).
- Shop in downtown Jersey City, near the Grove St station.
- Grab lunch on Washington St. in Hoboken.
